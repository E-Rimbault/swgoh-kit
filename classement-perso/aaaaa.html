Sabine Wren

Aggressive Phoenix attacker who permanently reduces defense and punishes slow enemies.

Light Side Attacker Mandalorian Phoenix Rebel Crew Members


Basic Attack: Hip Blaster

Deals physical damage to an enemy target twice.
Each of these attacks deals an additional hit if:
- the target's turn meter is below 70%.
- the target's turn meter is below 30%.
- if the target is suffering from debuffs.
The additional hits deal 75% less damage.

1st Special: Black Saber Strike (Cooldown: 2)

Deals physical damage to the enemy target and applies
armor shred for the rest of the battle.

2nd Special: Demolition (Cooldown: 5)

Deals physical damage to all enemies and applies Foresight for 2 turns.
The target also suffers Expose for 2 turns. For each active Mandalorian or Phoenix ally,
deals +15% more damage and exposes a random enemy for 2 turns.
Mandalorian and Phoenix allies gain a critical chance and attack bonus for 2 turns.
If this attack is a critical hit, Sabine’s cooldowns are reduced by 2. This attack cannot be countered or dodged.

Unique Ability: Counteroffensive

Sabine gains a +25% critical chance and +25% critical damage bonus.

Ship: Phantom II
