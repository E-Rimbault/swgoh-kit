Battle For Naboo

Story & Rules

STORY+
The Trade Federation has landed its armies on the peaceful planet of Naboo. Engage their forces both on land and in space as you weather the seemingly endless hordes of droid combatants. Making the correct tactical decisions is key to buying your fighters enough time to destroy the Droid Control Ship and seize victory.
RULES+
• Time Limit: 2d 23h
• Requirements: 5 Stars, Specific Units
• Roster Refresh: Never
• Launch Cost: 180K
• Max Attempts: 5

Faction Bonuses

Characters can obtain one or more of the following abilities during the raid.
Gungans
Shield Generator gains 5 Plasma Shielding. While there is an ally Shield Generator, Phalanx gains Taunt at the end of the turn if he didn't have it for 1 turn. Whenever an allied Shield Generator uses Plasma Pulse:

- Gungan allies gain Foresight for 1 turn
- A random Gungan ally gains a bonus turn
- All enemies are inflicted with Plasma Residue for 1 turn, which can't be resisted
Galactic Republic
Jedi Healers and Supports gain 50% Max Health and Jedi Healers and Tanks gain 100% Defense and 50% Max Protection. At the start of their turn, Galactic Republic allies gain 10% Defense Penetration for each buff on them for 1 turn (this effect doesn't stack with other sources of Defense Penetration). If the ally in the Leader slot is a Healer:

- Whenever a Jedi ally recovers Health they also recover 50% Protection
- Whenever a Jedi ally is critically hit they gain Critical Hit Immunity and 100 Speed for 1 turn and reduce their cooldowns by 1
- While a Jedi ally has Heal Over Time they have +50% Defense
- While a Jedi ally has both Heal Over Time and Potency Up they have 40% counter chance and deal bonus True damage to all enemies when using an ability
- Whenever an ally Jedi Healer uses a Special ability, dispel all buffs on target enemy
Separatists
Darth Sidious's Leader ability is active at max rank and also applies to Separatist allies when he is not in the Leader slot. Separatists and Sith allies have +100% Max Health, +25% Accuracy and Offense, +50% Potency, and +20 Speed (tripled for Darth Maul). Whenever a Separatist or Sith ally takes damage they gain 20% Defense (stacking) for 1 turn. Whenever an enemy is defeated by a Separatist or Sith ally, all allies recover 15% Health and Protection. Whenever a Sith ally inflicts a debuff, they also deal 5% Max Health damage to that enemy, which can't be evaded. Whenever a Separatist ally in the Leader slot uses a Special ability all Sith allies are called to assist.
Jar Jar Binks
This character has +25% Max Protection and Offense and +20% Mastery. Whenever this character inflicts a debuff, recover 10% Health and Protection and gain 10% Defense and Offense (stacking) for 1 turn.
Master Qui-Gon
This character has +25% Max Protection and Offense and +20% Mastery. Whenever this character inflicts a debuff, recover 10% Health and Protection and gain 10% Defense and Offense (stacking) for 1 turn.
Darth Maul
This character has +25% Max Protection and Offense and +20% Mastery. Whenever this character inflicts a debuff, recover 10% Health and Protection and gain 10% Defense and Offense (stacking) for 1 turn.
STAP
This character has +25% Max Protection and Offense and +20% Mastery. Whenever this character inflicts a debuff, recover 10% Health and Protection and gain 10% Defense and Offense (stacking) for 1 turn.
