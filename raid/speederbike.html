Speeder Bike Pursuit

Story & Rules

STORY+
Two of the Empire's elite Scout Troopers evade their enemies at breakneck speed through the forests of Endor. They throw caution to the wind as they weather your forces' attempts to damage, distract, and sideswipe them. The Scout Troopers will attempt to call powerful allies to help shake you off their tails, so you'll need to jam their comlinks to weaken these reinforcements.
RULES+
• Time Limit: 2d 23h
• Requirements: 5 Stars, Specific Units
• Roster Refresh: Never
• Launch Cost: 180K
• Max Attempts: 8

Faction Bonuses

Characters can obtain one or more of the following abilities during the raid.
Rebels
When this character uses a Basic ability, they repair stacks of Damage on all allies. Rebel characters gain a stack of Shield for every stack of Repair above max on self.
Imperial
This character starts the battle with stacks of Jam. When this character uses a Basic ability, they remove bonus stacks of Damage from target enemy equal to the number of stacks of Evade on all allies. Then, this character loses all stacks of Evade.
Ewok
When this character uses a Basic ability, they remove bonus stacks of Timber from target enemy equal to the number of stacks of Jam on all allies. Whenever an enemy is defeated with Timber, all Ewok allies gain stacks of Jam.
Strengths
This character is immune to Poisoned. Damage this character deals is decreased by 50%.

Whenever this character deals damage, it doesn't trigger Turn Meter gain on enemies, and they have a 75% chance to inflict Tenacity Down for 2 turns on all enemies.
Statistical Rework
This character will gain additional effects based on their start of combat stat values.

Granted Abilities+
Statistical Rework
Characters will gain additional benefits based on their individual stats at start of battle:

- Health: Provides stacks of Shield based on the character's Max Health (Shield: Blocks incoming stacks of Damage)

- 0-10,000 Health: Gain 5 stacks of Shield
- Every additional 10,000 Health up to 200,000: Gain 1 stack of Shield

- Protection: Provides stacks of Shield based on the character's Max Protection (Shield: Blocks incoming stacks of Damage)

- 0-10,000 Protection: Gain 5 stacks of Shield
- Every additional 10,000 Protection up to 200,000: Gain 1 stack of Shield

- Physical Accuracy: Remove additional stacks of Sideswipe for each stack of Sideswipe removed with an ability

- Below 5%: No effect
- 5%-14.99%: Remove 2 additional stacks of Sideswipe
- 15%-24.99%: Remove 4 additional stacks of Sideswipe
- 25% or higher: Remove 6 additional stacks of Sideswipe

- Physical Critical Avoidance: Remove additional stacks of Timber when removing Timber with an ability

- Below 25%: No effect
- 25%-49.99%: Remove 2 additional stacks of Timber
- 50%-74.99%: Remove 4 additional stacks of Timber
- 75%-99.99%: Remove 6 additional stacks of Timber
- 100% or higher: Remove 8 additional stacks of Timber

- Physical Critical Chance: Chance to reduce a random ally's cooldowns by 1 at start of turn

- 30% or lower: Gain a 10% chance to reduce an ally's cooldowns
- 30.1%-50%: Gain a 15% chance to reduce an ally's cooldowns
- 50.1%-75%: Gain a 20% chance to reduce an ally's cooldowns
- 75.1% or higher: Gain a 25% chance to reduce an ally's cooldowns

- Critical Damage: Gain Turn Meter when this character's cooldowns are reduced

- 179% or lower: No effect
- 180%-199%: Gain 10% Turn Meter
- 200%-219%: Gain 20% Turn Meter
- 220% or higher: Gain a 30% Turn Meter

- Attack Damage: Remove additional stacks of Damage for each stack of Damage removed with an ability

- 3000-5999: Remove 4 additional stacks of Damage
- 6000-9999: Remove 8 additional stacks of Damage
- 10000 or higher: Remove 12 additional stacks of Damage

- Armor: Block stacks of incoming Damage from enemy speeder bikes

- Below 637: No effect
- 637-1486: Block 1 incoming stack of Damage
- 1487 or higher: Block 2 incoming stacks of Damage

- Physical Evasion: Gain additional stacks of Evasion when gaining Evasion

- Below 5%: No effect
- 5%-9.99%: Gain 1 additional stack of Evasion
- 10%-14.99%: Gain 2 additional stacks of Evasion
- 15% or higher: Gain 3 additional stacks of Evasion

- Health Steal: Repair stacks of Damage on self when removing 16 or more stacks of Damage on an enemy

- Below 10%: No effect
- 10%-24%: Repair 1 stack of Damage on self
- 25%-39%: Repair 2 stacks of Damage on self
- 40% or higher: Repair 3 stacks of Damage on self

- Potency: Remove additional stacks of Distract for each stack of Distract removed by an ability

- Below 30%: Remove 2 additional stacks of Distract
- 30%-49%: Remove 4 additional stacks of Distract
- 50%-79%: Remove 6 additional stacks of Distract
- 80% or higher: Remove 8 additional stacks of Distract

- Tenacity: Reduce the cost of successfully evading an incoming attack

- Below 20%: No effect
- 20%-39%: Successfully evading consumes 1 less stack of Evasion
- 40%-59%: Successfully evading consumes 2 less stacks of Evasion
- 60%-79%: Successfully evading consumes 3 less stacks of Evasion
- 80%-99%: Successfully evading consumes 4 less stacks of Evasion
- 100% or higher: Successfully evading consumes 5 less stacks of Evasion
