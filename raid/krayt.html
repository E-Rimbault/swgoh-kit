Krayt Dragon Hunt

Story & Rules

STORY+
Tusken nomads tell tales to their children of the legendary Krayt Dragon of Tatooine, which swims through the dunes of the desert as if they were water. It's cavernous gullet and acidic venom have been the end of many moisture farmers, Jawas, and Tuskens alike.

Hero Units: The Mandalorian (Beskar Armor)
RULES+
• Time Limit: 2d 23h
• Requirements: 5 Stars, Specific Units
• Roster Refresh: Never
• Launch Cost: 180K
• Max Attempts: 5

Faction Bonuses

Characters can obtain one or more of the following abilities during the raid.
Hero Units
This character has +100% Max Health and Max Protection. At the start of this character's turn, they dispel all debuffs on themselves and reduce the cooldown of FireBallista and Replant Explosives by 20%. When this character uses Fire Ballista or Replant Explosives, reduce its cooldown by 40%.

The first time each turn this character attacks out of turn, they have a 75% chance to inflict a stack of Damage Over Time on target enemy for 1 turn. This character has +30% Potency and deals 5% more damage per stack of Momentum.

This character is immune to Poisoned. Damage this character deals is reduced by 50%. Whenever this character damages an enemy, it doesn't trigger Turn Meter gain, and they have a 75% chance to inflict Tenacity Down for 2 turns.

These effects don't stack with the same effect from other Faction Bonus abilities.
Hutt Cartel
Whenever a Hutt Cartel ally damages a Bundle of explosives, it immediately detonates and deals double damage to the Krayt Dragon.

At the start of this character's turn, reduce the cooldown of Replant Explosives by 20%. When this character uses Replant Explosives, reduce its cooldown by 40%.
Jawas
This character has +100% Max Health and Max Protection. At the start of this character's turn, they dispel all debuffs on themselves. Whenever a Jawa ally inflictsa Thermal Detonator on an enemy, damage that enemy receives from Jawas and Thermal Detonators is increased by 9% for each active Jawa (stacking).
Mandalorians
This character is immune to Poisoned. Damage this character deals is decreased by 50%.

Whenever this character deals damage, it doesn't trigger Turn Meter gain on enemies, and they have a 75% chance to inflict Tenacity Down for 2 turns on all enemies.
Old Republic
Whenever an enemy is inflicted with a stack of Damage Over Time, Old Republic allies deal 1% more damage until the end of battle (stacking) for each active Old Republic ally. The first time each turn this character attacks out of turn, they have a 75% chance to inflict a stack of Damage Over Time on target enemy for 1 turn.
Tuskens
At the start of this character's turn, reduce the cooldown of Fire Ballista by 20%. When this character uses Fire Ballista, reduce its cooldown by 40%.

This character has +30% Potency and deals 5% more damage per stack of Momentum.

Abilities

Granted Abilities+
Fire Ballista
Cooldown Speed: 100

Deal true damage to target enemy 2 times and inflict Daze, Stagger, and Stun for 1 turn 2 times. This attack can't be evaded or resisted.

If a non-Attacker used this ability, it deals 15% more damage for each stack of Momentum they had.
Replant Explosives
Cooldown Speed: 100

Plant 2 Bundles of Explosives around the Krayt Dragon (max 3).
Krayt Dragon
+
Crushing Maw
Deal Physical damage to target enemy and Daze them for 2 turns. Deal Physical damage to all other enemies.

Enraged: Stun target enemy for 2 turns. Daze all enemies for 2 turns.
Corrosive Venom Spray
Instantly defeat any enemies that are currently Swallowed. Dispel all buffs on all enemies. Deal Special damage to all enemies and inflict Defense Down, Expose, Poisoned, Speed Down, and Vulnerability for 2 turns.

Poisoned: -50% Health and Protection recovery; can't recover Health with Health Steal; takes damage each turn equal to 75% of the total Health and Protection recovered since their last turn for each time Health or Protection was recovered since their last turn

Enraged: Defense Down, Expose, Poisoned, Speed Down, and Vulnerability can't be dispelled or resisted.
Swallow Whole
Swallow target enemy and remove their status effects. The Krayt Dragon gains 20 stacks of Defiance. While an enemy is Swallowed, the Krayt Dragon's Speed is reduced by 25%, and its Turn Meter can't be reduced. When Defiance is removed, the Swallowed enemy is returned. If the Swallowed enemy is the last enemy, the battle immediately ends. This ability starts each battle on cooldown with 1 turn remaining.

Swallowed: An enemy has been Swallowed; they will be spit out when all Defiance stacks have been removed, and they will be instantly defeated if Corrosive Venom Spray is used while they are Swallowed

Enraged: If this is the first ability used this turn, reset its cooldown..
Dune Burrow
Gain 20% bonus Protection and the granted ability Violent Eruption until it is used or until this bonus Protection is removed. While the Krayt Dragon has this bonus Protection, it can't lose Defiance, Health, or Turn Meter (excluding from Bundles of Explosives), its Speed is reduced by 25%, and damage it receives from Bundles of Explosives is increased by 400%. This ability starts each battle on cooldown with 2 turns remaining.

Enraged: Damage the Krayt Dragon receives from Bundles of Explosives is no longer increased.

Violent Eruption: Deal true damage to all enemies, dealing more damage based on the amount of bonus Protection remaining, and Stun all enemies for 1 turn. Then, the Krayt Dragon loses all bonus Protection. This attack can't be evaded.
Apex Predator
Damage the Krayt Dragon receives is increased by 75% while it doesn't have bonus Protection. Whenever the Krayt Dragon is damaged while it doesn't have bonus Protection and it doesn't have an enemy Swallowed, it gains 6% Turn Meter.

Whenever any character takes a turn, the Krayt Dragon gains 1% Enrage. Whenever the Krayt Dragon takes a turn, it gains an additional 4% Enrage and 4 stacks of Defiance. Whenever an enemy removes Turn Meter from the Krayt Dragon, it gains 1% Enrage for each 10% Turn Meter removed. When it reaches 100%, the Krayt Dragon becomes Enraged. The battle immediately ends after the Krayt Dragon has taken 5 turns while Enraged or if all active enemies are Swallowed.

For each 5,000 points earned this battle after 50,000 points, this unit gains 3% Offense (stacking) until the end of battle. This bonus increases by an additional 3% each time.
Fearsome Defiance
When inflicted with the following debuffs, lose stacks of Defiance and remove those debuffs. If no stacks of Defiance are present, this unit is immune to these debuffs.

Defiance: Lose stacks when inflicted with the following debuffs:
Stun and Fear: 8 stacks
Ability Block: 6 stacks
Daze: 5 stacks
Blind: 3 stacks
Stagger: 2 stacks
Tenacity Down: 1 stack
Bundle of Explosives
+
Calculated Impact
Detonates after receiving damage 5 times, dealing damage to the Krayt Dragon and other Bundles of Explosives. If the Krayt Dragon has bonus Protection and isn't Enraged, also reduce its Turn Meter by 15%. Immune to all status effects. When this unit is defeated, dispel all debuffs on all enemies.
