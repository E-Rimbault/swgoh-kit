Mob Enforcer

Scoundrel Support with enemy debuff ability and a thermal detonator.


Dark Side Soutien Hutt Cartel Scoundrel


Basic Attack: Dirty Fighting

Deal Physical damage to target enemy and inflict Defense Down for 2 turns. If the ally in the Leader slot is Hutt Cartel and the target already had Defense Down, inflict Daze, Expose, and Vulnerable for 2 turns instead.

Special Attack : Thermal Detonator (cooldown: 3)

Deal Physical damage to target enemy and detonate all Thermal Detonators on them. Then, inflict a Thermal Detonator on target enemy for 2 turns with a 40% chance for Mob Enforcer to gain a bonus turn. Instead, if this ability defeats an enemy, Mob Enforcer gains a bonus turn.

Unique Ability : Underworld Moxie

At the start of the encounter, Mob Enforcer gains 25% Max Health for each Scoundrel ally, 25% Max Protection for each Bounty Hunter ally, and 10 Speed for each Hutt Cartel ally. Whenever an ally falls below 30% Health, Mob Enforcer gains Offense Up for 2 turns and the cooldown of Thermal Detonator is reduced by 1.
