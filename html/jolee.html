Jolee Bindo

Potent Jedi Healer with high durability and resistance to disruption


Light Side Healer Jedi Old Republic


Basic Attack: No Other Options

Deal Physical damage to target enemy and recover 30% Health. If this attack deals any damage, reduce the cooldowns of Jolee's other abilities by 1.

Special Attack : Eh, You Do It (cooldown: 3)

Another target ally Assists. Heal that ally for 30% of Jolee's Max Health, then all other Jedi allies recover half that amount.

Special Attack : That Looks Pretty Bad (cooldown: 5)

Dispel all debuffs on target ally and they recover 40% Health. Then, Revive all defeated Jedi allies at 80% Health and grant those allies Crit Immunity for 5 turns.

Unique Ability : Don't Interrupt Me!

Jolee has +100% Tenacity and is immune to Turn Meter reduction effects. In addition, at the start of the battle, he gains bonus Critical Avoidance equal to half of his Tenacity.

Unique Ability : Experience and Wisdom

Jolee has +30% Max Health. Whenever he uses an ability that targets an ally, he grants that ally Protection Up for 2 turns equal to 30% of his Max Health.
