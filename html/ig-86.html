IG-86 Sentinel Droid

Droid Attacker with a powerful Droid Assist and devastating Criticals


Darkside Attacker Droïd


Basic Attack: Precision Strike

Deal Physical damage to target enemy with an additional 50% chance of a Critical Hit.

Special Attack : Assassin Droid Tactics (cooldown: 2)

Deal Physical damage to target enemy and call a random ally to Assist. If the Assisting ally is a Droid, both attackers have an additional 75% Critical Damage.

Unique Ability : Droid Squad Tactics

IG-86 gains 4.5% Critical Chance for each living Droid ally. In addition, he has +20% Critical Damage.
