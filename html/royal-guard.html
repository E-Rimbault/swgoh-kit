Royal Guard

Versatile Empire Tank that punishes enemies for attacking other allies


Dark Side Tank Empire Crew Members

Ship: Emperor's Shuttle



Basic Attack: Force Pike

Deal Physical damage to target enemy with a 50% chance to inflict Speed Down for 2 turns. If Royal Guard has more than 50% Health, Stun the target for 1 turn.

Special Attack : Imperial Phalanx (cooldown: 3)

All Dark Side allies gain Defense Up and Heal Over Time for 2 turns and Empire allies gain Health Up and Retribution for 2 turns. Royal Guard gains Taunt for 1 turn.

Unique Ability : Unyielding Defender

At the start of the encounter, if the allied Leader is Empire, Royal Guard gains Max Health equal to 5% of the allied Leader's Max Health and then doubles his Health until the end of the encounter.

At the end of each turn, if another ally took damage that turn, all Empire allies recover 6% Health. Royal Guard Taunts for 1 turn whenever another ally falls below 50% Health.
