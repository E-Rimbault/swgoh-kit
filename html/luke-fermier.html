Luke Skywalker (Farmboy)

Attacker that can boost his own Critical Rate, then disable enemies with Critical Hits


Light Side Attacker Leader Rebel


Basic Attack: Frontier Marksmanship

Deal Physical Damage to target enemy. If this attack scores a Critical Hit, gain 40% Turn Meter and inflict Damage Over Time for 3 turns.

Special Attack : Bullseye (cooldown: 3)

Deal Physical damage to target enemy and inflict Defense Down and Speed Down for 2 turns. If this attack scores a Critical Hit, Stun the target for 1 turn. Otherwise, dispel all buffs on the target.

Leader Ability: A New Hope

All allies gain 50% Tenacity. Whenever an ally Resists a detrimental effect they gain Advantage for 2 turns.

Unique Ability : Draw a Bead

Luke has +25% Critical Chance. At the start of each of his turns, Luke gains 10% Critical Damage (stacking) until the end of the encounter.

Omicron Boost : While in Territory Wars: Luke gains 30% Defense, Max Health, and Tenacity. At the start of each of his turns, Luke gains 10% Defense and Offense (stacking) until the end of the encounter. Whenever Luke scores a critical hit during his turn, he gains 20% Turn Meter, and Critical Damage Up and Offense Up for 2 turns. When Luke is prevented from using an ability during his turn, he recovers 50% Health and Protection.
