Geonosian Soldier

Aggressive Attacker who calls assists and grants powerful offensive bonuses to Geonosian allies


Dark Side Attacker Geonosian Separatist Crew Members

Ship: Geonosian Soldier's Starfighter



Basic Attack: Aggressive Advance

Deal Physical damage to target enemy and inflict Tenacity Down for 2 turns.

Special Attack : Swarm (cooldown: 3)

Deal Physical damage to target enemy and call a random ally to assist. If the assisting ally is Geonosian, both attackers deal 25% more damage.

Hive Mind bonus: Instead, select the ally who assists.

Unique Ability : Hive Tactics

Geonosian allies have +15% Critical Chance. Geonosian Soldier gains 25% Turn Meter when scoring a critical hit.

Hive Mind bonus: Geonosian allies have +35% Defense Penetration.
