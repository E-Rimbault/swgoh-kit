Princess Leia

Well-rounded Rebel Attacker with extra attacks, Stealth, and team Critical bonuses.


Light Side Attacker Leader Rebel


Basic Attack: Hair Trigger

Deal Physical damage to target enemy with a 70% chance to attack a second time and a 55% chance to attack a third time. Each attack has a 50% chance to grant 10% Turn Meter.

Special Attack : Rebel Tactics (cooldown: 3)

Leia gains Stealth and Critical Damage Up for 3 turns and gains 50% Turn Meter. If Leia already had Stealth, she also gains Offense Up and Foresight for 3 turns and gains double Turn Meter.

Leader Ability: Heart of the Rebellion

Rebel allies gain 20% Critical Chance.

Unique Ability : Against All Odds

Whenever Leia attacks, she has a 55% chance to grant all allies Critical Chance Up for 2 turns, and recovers 5% Health and 5% Protection.

Omicron Boost : While in Grand Arenas: Whenever Leia attacks, she has a 55% chance to grant all Rebel allies Retribution for 2 turns and a 55% chance to grant all Rebel allies Tenacity Up for 2 turns, and all other Rebel allies recover 5% Health and Protection.
