Ugnaught

Anti-Droid Support with AoE damage and high Evasion against Droids


Light Side Support


Basic Attack: Break Open

Deal Physical damage to target enemy. This attack deals 100% more damage to Droids and inflicts Defense Down on them for 2 turns.

Special Attack : Droid Experts (cooldown: 3)

Deal Special damage to all enemies with a 75% chance to Stun Droids for 1 turn.

Unique Ability : One Step Ahead

Ugnaught gains 9% Evasion for each Droid enemy and gains 25% Turn Meter whenever he Evades an attack.
