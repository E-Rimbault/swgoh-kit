Clone Sergeant - Phase I

Clone Attacker with AoE damage and Turn Meter Reduction


Light Side Attacker Clone Trooper Galactic Republic Crew member


Basic Attack: Z-6 Rotary Blaster

Deal Physical damage to target enemy and gain 50% Turn Meter on a critical hit.

Special Attack: Suppressive Fire (cooldown: 3)

Deal Physical damage to all enemies and remove 50% of their Turn Meters.

Unique Ability: Concentrated Fire

Clone Sergeant Phase I gains 4.5% Critical Chance for each living Clone ally. In addition, he has +10% Critical Damage and Offense Up for 2 turns whenever he critically hits.
