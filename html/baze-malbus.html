Baze Malbus

Fearsome Rebel Tank who Taunts, dispels buffs, and inflicts debuffs


Light Side Tank Rebel Rebel Fighter Rogue One


Basic Attack: Heavy Repeater Cannon

Deal Physical damage to target enemy with a 70% chance to inflict Speed Down for 3 turns. If Chirrut Îmwe is present, he gains 10% Turn Meter.

Special Attack : Unload (cooldown: 3)

Deal Physical damage to all enemies and Dispel all buffs on them. Empire enemies and enemies who were not buffed take bonus damage equal to 25% of their Max Health. If Chirrut Îmwe is present, he is called to Assist.

Special Attack : Fierce Reprisal (cooldown: 3)

Deal Physical damage to target enemy and dispel all buffs on them. For each buff dispelled, inflict the opposite debuff, if any, for 2 turns.

Unique Ability : For Jedha

Baze Malbus gains Taunt and Retribution for 1 turn at the start of each encounter, whenever an enemy is defeated, and whenever Chirrut Îmwe falls below 100% Health. Baze Malbus gains 5% Turn Meter whenever an enemy gains a buff.

Unique Ability : Dauntless Courage

Baze Malbus gains Max Health equal to 20% of his Max Health. Chirrut Îmwe also gains that much Max Health.
