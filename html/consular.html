Jedi Consular

Well-rounded Jedi Healer with a mix of offense and defense


Light Side Healer Galactic Républic Jedi Jedi Vanguard Crew Members


Basic Attack: Saber Strike

Deal Physical damage to target enemy with a 50% chance to reduce Jedi Consular's Cooldowns by 1.

Special Attack: Jedi Healing (cooldown: 3)

Each ally recovers Health equal to 40% of Jedi Consular's Max Health, with a 50% chance of granting Jedi Consular 25% Turn Meter.

Special Attack: Attack as Defense (cooldown: 3)

Deal Special damage to target enemy and recover Health equal to 30% of the damage dealt. This attack has a 50% chance to ignore the target's Armor.
