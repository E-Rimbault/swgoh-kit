Magmatrooper

Resilient Attacker that recovers quickly from debuffs and deals potent AoE damage


Dark Side Attacker Empire Imperial Trooper


Basic Attack: Line of Fire

Deal Physical Damage to target enemy and deal damage to a random enemy. The secondary attack deals 20% less damage.

Special Attack : Thermal Imploder (cooldown: 4)

Deal Physical Damage to all enemies and remove 30% Turn Meter, with a 70% chance to remove another 30% Turn Meter. This attack ignores Armor.

Unique Ability : Hazard Training

Magmatrooper has +30% Tenacity and gains 70% Turn Meter the first time he Resists a detrimental effect or suffers a debuff each turn.

While Magmatrooper is active, Imperial Trooper allies have +30% Tenacity.
