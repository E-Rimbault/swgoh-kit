Ewok Scout

Powerful single-target Attacker that becomes evasive with Ewok allies


Light Side Attacker Ewok


Basic Attack: Ewok Ambush

Deal Physical damage to target enemy with a 55% chance to remove 50% Turn Meter.

Special Attack : Rushing Attack (cooldown: 3)

Deal Physical damage to target enemy with an additional 50% chance of a Critical Hit and call a random ally to Assist.

Unique Ability : Tribal Tactics

Ewok Scout gains 5% Evasion for each living Ewok ally and gains 30% Turn Meter whenever he Evades an attack or lands a Critical Hit.
