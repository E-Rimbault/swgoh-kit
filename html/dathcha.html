Dathcha

Versatile Support able to Ability Block and Stealth, effective against Droids or with Jawas


Light Side Support Leader Jawa

Basic Attack: Hit and Run

Deal Physical damage to target enemy with a 60% chance to gain Stealth for 2 turns.

1st Special: Scout Instincts (cooldown: 4)

Deal Special damage to all enemies with a 50% chance to inflict Ability Block for 1 turn and a 50% chance to Stun Droids for 1 turn.

Leader Ability: Decommission

Whenever an ally damages a Droid, they have an 80% chance to inflict Defense Down for 3 turns and a 25% chance to remove 25% Turn Meter.

Unique Ability: Droid Hunter

Dathcha gains 4.5% Critical Chance for each living Jawa ally. In addition, he has +15% Critical Damage.
