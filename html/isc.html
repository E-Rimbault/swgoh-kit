Imperial Super Commando

An Empire Attacker with counter mechanics who dispels buffs and applies buff immunity


Dark Side Attacker Empire Mandalorian Crew Member


Basic Attack: Seize the Advantage

Deal Physical damage to target enemy with a 70% chance to inflict Offense Down for 2 turns and attack again if the target had less than 50% Turn Meter.

Special Attack : Superior Tactics (cooldown: 2)

Deal Physical damage to target enemy, dispel all buffs on them, and inflict Buff Immunity for 2 turns.

Unique Ability : Upper Hand

Imperial Super Commando has +100% counter chance and gains +15% Offense for each enemy with no buffs.

If all allies were Dark Side Mandalorians or Empire at the start of battle:

- Whenever a Dark Side Mandalorian ally attacks out of turn, they deal additional True damage and recover 10% Protection

- Whenever Imperial Super Commando or Gar Saxon attack out of turn, the other attacks as well (limit once per turn)

Unique Ability : Super Commando Beskar Armor

Imperial Super Commando and Gar Saxon gain 30% Defense, 50% Critical Avoidance, 20% Max Health, and 20% Max Protection.

The first time Imperial Super Commando or Gar Saxon would be defeated, they gain Damage Immunity for 2 turns instead.
