Anakin's Eta-2 Starfighter

Aggressive Jedi Attacker that bolsters Offense and retaliates when allies are in trouble


Light Side Attacker Galactic Republic Jedi
Jedi Knight Anakin


Basic Attack: Locked On

Deal Physical damage to target enemy and inflict Target Lock for 2 turns. This attack can't be Evaded.

Target Lock: Grant another random ally Valor for 2 turns.

Crew Ability - Jedi Knight Anakin : Surge All Power

Jedi and Clone allies gain +30% Offense (doubled if they have Valor). Whenever another ally falls below 100% Health or is defeated, Anakin's Starfighter gains 100% Turn Meter and its next attack deals 50% more damage. Additionally, Anakin's Starfighter immediately regains Valor for 2 turns whenever it expires.

Special Attack : Impeding Assault (cooldown: 3)

Deal Physical damage to all enemies and inflict Healing Immunity and Buff Immunity on target enemy for 2 turns. Anakin's Starfighter gains 20% Protection Up (stacking) for 2 turns for each Critical Hit.

Reinforcement Ability: Aggressive Approach

Enter Battle: Grant all allies Accuracy Up for 2 turns and all Galactic Republic allies Offense Up for 2 turns. Inflict Expose on all enemies for 2 turns, which can't be Evaded.
