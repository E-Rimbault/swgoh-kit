Tusken Shaman

Offensive Healer that can also boost Offense and rapidly stack Damage Over Time effects


Dark Side Healer Tusken


Basic Attack: Desert Toxins

Deal Physical Damage to target enemy and inflict Damage Over Time for 2 turns. This attack inflicts an additional Damage Over Time effect if that target was already suffering a negative status effect.

Special Attack : Rite of Savagery (cooldown: 4)

All allies gain Offense Up for 1 turn and recover Health equal to 20% of their Max Health, plus an additional 10% for each enemy suffering a negative status effect

Unique Ability : Master Storyteller

Whenever any unit is defeated, all Tusken allies gain 25% Turn Meter and recover 10% of their Max Health.
