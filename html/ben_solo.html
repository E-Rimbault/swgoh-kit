Ben Solo

A powerful Force user whose internal struggles have long placed him on the edge of light and dark


Light Side Attacker Unaligned Force User


Basic Attack: A Shadow Within

Deal Special damage to target enemy and inflict Defense Down for 1 turn. If the target enemy is a Tank, this attack deals 100% more damage. If the target enemy is an Attacker or Support, inflict Offense Down and Potency Down for 1 turn. If the target enemy is a Healer, inflict Speed Down for 1 turn.

Special Attack : Obscured (cooldown: 4)

Deal Special damage to target enemy, remove 100% Turn Meter and, if there is any version of Rey in the allied Leader slot, Blind the target enemy for 2 turns. Gain Offense Up for 2 turns.

Omicron Boost : While in Grand Arenas if all allies were Light Side at the start of battle:
When this ability is used, Ben Solo’s cooldowns are reduced by 1. If Galactic Legend Rey is in the allied Leader slot, this attack instead deals true damage and inflicts Healing Immunity for 4 turns, which can’t be copied, dispelled, evaded, or resisted.

Special Attack : Family Legacy (cooldown: 4)

Deal Special damage to target enemy and inflict Armor Shred until the end of the encounter. If all allies were Light Side at the start of battle, dispel all buffs from all enemies and deal true damage again to all enemies. All allies gain 5% Turn Meter (max 25%) for each enemy damaged by this ability.

If this ability defeats an enemy, Ben Solo gains 10% Offense (stacking, max 50%) and all other Light Side allies gain 5% Offense (stacking, max 25%) until the end of battle.

This attack can't be evaded.

Unique Ability: Redeemed

At the start of battle, if all of Ben Solo’s allies were Light Side and there is any version of Rey in the allied Leader slot, Ben Solo gains 15% Max Health and 10 Speed for each Light Side ally (excluding summons). Ben Solo ignores taunt effects during his turn.

Omicron Boost : While in Grand Arenas:
Ben Solo's attacks ignore Protection.

Unique Ability : Force Dyad

If all allies were Light Side at the start of battle and there is any version of Rey in the allied Leader slot: All allies gain Instant Defeat Immunity for 1 turn at the start of each encounter, which can’t be copied, dispelled, or prevented. While Ben Solo is active, Rey can’t be defeated. Ben Solo can’t be defeated while Rey is above 50% Health.

Omicron Boost : While in Grand Arenas, if Galactic Legend Rey is in the allied Leader slot:
If the enemy in the Leader slot begins their turn with Ben Solo defeated and Ben still has an active ally, he revives with 51% Health and all allies gain Instant Defeat Immunity for the rest of the encounter, which can’t be copied, dispelled, or prevented.
