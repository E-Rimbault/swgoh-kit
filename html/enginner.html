Jawa Enginner

High-speed Jawa Healer with a Critical Chance buff and both Jawa and Droid synergy


Light Side Healer Jawa

Basic Attack: Miniaturized Detonator

Apply a Detonator to target enemy that detonates after 1 turn, dealing damage.

1st Special: Patch Up (cooldown: 4)

Jawa Engineer revives a random Droid ally at 1% Health, and then all allies recover 30% of their Max Health.

2nd Special: Recalibrate (cooldown: 5)

All allies gain Critical Chance Up for 2 turns. Jawa and Droid allies also gain 45% Turn Meter.

Unique Ability: Crunch Time

Jawa Engineer has +10 Speed for each Jawa or Droid ally.
