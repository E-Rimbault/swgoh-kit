Chopper

Surly Phoenix Support who Taunts and Dispels, and can recover Phoenix cooldowns


Light Side Support Droïde Phoenix Rebel Spectre Crew Member

Ship: Phantom II



Basic Attack: Reckless Fling

Deal Physical damage to target enemy and grant a random Phoenix or Droid ally Defense Up, Offense Up, or Speed Up at random for 2 turns, with a 60% chance to also reduce that ally's cooldowns by 1.

Special Attack : Cantankerous Clanker (cooldown: 3)

Chopper gains Taunt for 1 turn. While he has this Taunt buff, Chopper gains 30% Evasion.

Special Attack : Metal Menace (cooldown: 4)

Dispel all buffs on target enemy. For each buff dispelled this way, all enemies lose 10% Turn Meter and Chopper recovers 25% Protection. Droid targets are also Stunned for 1 turn, which can't be evaded.

Unique Ability : Maintenance Protocols

At the start of his turn, Chopper recovers 15% Health. Whenever he is damaged by an attack, he gains Protection Up (15%) for 2 turns if he doesn't already have Protection Up. Chopper has a 30% chance to assist when another Phoenix or Droid ally uses an ability during their turn (this assist is not shared with Phoenix allies).
