Jaxxon

A Lepi Smuggler who made his way across the galaxy for riches, and maybe also some heroic antics along the way


Light Side Attacker Mercenary Scoundrel Smuggler


Basic Attack: Blaster's Up, Doc

Deal Physical damage to target enemy twice and inflict Vulnerable for 1 turn.

Prepared: Jaxxon attacks again, and if it is his turn, all Prepared allies gain 25% Turn Meter for each attack.

Special Attack : Hop 'Til You Drop (cooldown: 3)

Deal Physical damage to target enemy and inflict Off Balance for 2 turns.

Prepared: Stun target enemy for 1 turn.

Special Attack : Fluffle Shuffle (cooldown: 4)

Deal Physical damage to all enemies. If the enemy Leader is Dark Side, this attack deals 20% more damage for each Prepared ally. Otherwise, this attack deals 10% more damage for each Prepared ally.

Jaxxon has a 25% chance to use Blaster's Up, Doc. If any version of Lando Calrissian is an active ally, this chance becomes 100%.

Prepared: This attack can't be countered or evaded.

Unique Ability : Starship Down

Jaxxon has +20% Critical Chance, Max Health, and +20 Speed. If any version of Lando Calrissian is an active ally, Jaxxon and every version of Lando gain 50% Critical Chance, Critical Damage, Max Health, and all allies are immune to Speed Down for 1 turn.

While Jaxxon is active, Prepared allies have +50% Defense. Whenever a Prepared ally loses Prepared, they gain 10% Turn Meter.

Whenever any version of Lando Calrissian uses a Special ability, they dispel all debuffs on all allies, and all allies recover 20% Health and Protection.

The first time Jaxxon would be reduced to 1% Health, he Stealths for 2 turns and recovers 100% Health and Protection.

If all allies are Scoundrels, whenever Jaxxon scores a critical hit, he gains 20% Critical Damage (stacking, max 200%) until the next time he doesn't critically hit.

Omicron Boost : While in Territory Battles: Scoundrel allies have +50% Max Health and Max Protection and +50 Speed.

Whenever a Prepared ally loses Prepared, they gain Prepared. Whenever a Scoundrel ally gains Prepared, they gain 30% bonus Protection and Defense Penetration (stacking) until the end of battle.

At the start of each encounter, Scoundrel allies gain Prepared if they didn't have it and take a bonus turn.

Enemies defeated by Scoundrel allies can't be revived.
