Coruscant Underworld Police

Support that shuts down enemies with Stuns and Offense Down


Light Side Support Galactic Republic


Basic Attack: Non-Lethal Takedown

Deal Physical damage to target enemy with a 50% chance to Stun the target for one turn.

Special Attack: Non-Lethal Crowd Control (cooldown: 3)

Deal Physical damage to all enemies with a 70% chance to inflict Offense Down for 2 turns.

Unique Ability: Non-Lethal Specialist

Coruscant Underworld Police gains 30% Potency. In addition, he gains 20% Turn Meter whenever he inflicts a negative status effect.
