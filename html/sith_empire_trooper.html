Sith Empire Trooper

Sith Tank who Taunts and ignores enemy Protection


Dark Side Tank Sith Sith Empire Crew Members Loading Era...

Ship: Mark VI Interceptor



Basic Attack: Disruptor Blast

Deal Physical damage to target enemy with a 70% chance to inflict Defense Down for 3 turns. If the target was already debuffed, ignore their Protection

Special Attack : Crimson Barrage (cooldown: 3)

Deal Physical damage to all enemies with a 70% chance to inflict Offense Down for 2 turns. Then gain Protection Up equal to 5% for each debuffed enemy for 2 turns. This attack ignores Protection.

Unique Ability : Vaiken's Legacy

Sith Empire Trooper has +100% Defense.

Whenever a Sith ally uses a Special ability or falls below 50% Health, Sith Empire Trooper gains Defense Up for 2 turns. If he already had Defense Up, he gains Taunt for 2 turns. If he already had Taunt, he gains Retribution for 2 turns.

Sith Empire Trooper gains Defense Up and Taunt for 2 turns at the start of each encounter.
