Bo-katan (Mand'alor)

A brave leader of the Mandalorians who wields the Darksaber and leads her allies into battle


Light Side Attacker Leader Mandalorian


Basic Attack: Frontal Assault

Deal Physical and True damage to target enemy, which can't be evaded, and dispel all buffs on them. During Bo-Katan (Mand'alor)'s turn, all Light Side Mandalorian allies gain Speed Up for 2 turns.

Special Attack : Darksaber Flourish (cooldown: 3)

Deal Physical damage to target enemy and inflict Armor Shred until the end of battle. Deal an additional instance of True damage to target enemy, which can't be evaded, and Stun them for 1 turn. If Bo-Katan (Mand'alor) is debuffed, deal True damage an additional time to the target enemy, which can't be evaded. Otherwise, reduce the cooldown of this ability by 1.

Special Attack : Reinforcements Have Arrived (cooldown: 4)

Deal Physical damage to target enemy. Dispel all debuffs from all Light Side Mandalorian allies, call them all to assist twice, dealing 50% less damage, and they all gain 3 stacks of Ancestral Resolve: (max 100) until the end of the encounter, which can't be copied, dispelled or prevented.

Ancestral Resolve: +2% Defense and +1% Max Health; whenever this character deals True damage, deal additional True damage based on the number of stacks, which can't be evaded

Omicron Boost : While in Grand Arenas:
Light Side Mandalorian allies gain an additional 2 stacks of Ancestral Resolve. If another Light Side Mandalorian ally wasn't able to assist, they instead gain a bonus turn.

Leader Ability: Way of the Mandalore

Light Side Mandalorian allies gain 40% Defense, Max Health and Offense. Light Side Mandalorian allies gain 5% Defense (stacking, max 30%) for each debuff on them. Whenever a Light Side Mandalorian ally deals True damage, dispels a debuff, or resists a debuff, all Light Side Mandalorian allies gain 1 stack of Ancestral Resolve (max 100) until the end of the encounter, which can't be copied, dispelled, or prevented. Whenever an ally attacks an enemy inflicted with Armor Shred, all Light Side Mandalorian Tank allies Taunt for 1 turn (max 2 times per turn).

Omicron Boost : While in Grand Arenas:
Light Side Mandalorian allies gain 50% Mastery, Max Health and 30 Speed. At the start of each encounter, Light Side Mandalorian Tank allies Taunt for 2 turns. If there are other active enemies (excluding summons), the first Light Side Mandalorian ally that is defeated each battle is revived and recovers 50% Health and Protection. All Light Side Mandalorian allies gain 1 additional stack of Ancestral Resolve whenever a Light Side Mandalorian ally deals True damage, dispels a debuff, or resists a debuff.

Unique Ability : For Mandalore!

Bo-Katan (Mand'alor) gains Tenacity Up for 1 turn at the start of each encounter, which can't be dispelled or prevented.

Bo-Katan (Mand'alor) has +10 Speed (stacking) for each Stunned Light Side Mandalorian ally, and True damage she deals from Ancestral Resolve is tripled. This damage is then doubled against enemies with bonus Protection.

If all allies were Light Side Mandalorian at the start of battle: Bo-Katan (Mand'alor) takes reduced damage from percent Health damage effects and recovers 50% Health whenever an ally is defeated for the first time (excluding summoned allies). She gains 50% Defense (stacking, max 200%) for each debuff on her, and an additional 200% Defense (stacking) for each other Light Side Mandalorian ally who has not yet been defeated.

Omicron Boost : While in Grand Arenas:
Bo-Katan (Mand'alor) is immune to Daze. If all allies were Light Side Mandalorian at the start of battle: Bo-Katan (Mand'alor) ignores Taunt effects, and whenever an ally is defeated for the first time, she gains 20% of their Max Health and Offense (stacking) until the end of the battle.
