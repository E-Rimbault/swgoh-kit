Kylo Ren's Command Shuttle

Durable First Order Support with unparalleled ability to manipulate allied Turn Meter


Dark Side Support First Order

Kylo Ren
Captain Phasma
First Order Stormtrooper


Basic Attack: Defensive Fire

Deal Physical damage to target enemy and gain Protection Up (50%) for 2 turns.

Crew Ability - Kylo Ren : Surprise Raid (cooldown: 4)

Target ally gains 100% Turn Meter and Advantage for 1 turn, and their cooldowns are reduced by 1, but they lose 20% Protection.

Crew Ability - Captain Phasma : Strike Team (cooldown: 4)

Target ally gains 100% Turn Meter and Advantage for 1 turn, and their cooldowns are reduced by 1, but they lose 20% Protection.

Crew Ability - First Order Stormtrooper : Zeal of the First Order

Kylo Ren's Command Shuttle has +40 Speed while it has Protection and +100% Counter Chance while it doesn't. When another First Order ally takes damage, Kylo Ren's Command Shuttle gains 15% Turn Meter and all his cooldowns are reduced by 1.

Special Ability : Advanced Jamming Array (cooldown : 4)

Dispel all buffs on target enemy, with a 70% chance to remove 30% Turn Meter and inflict Healing Immunity for 2 turns, which can't be Evaded. Also inflict Ability Block on target enemy for 2 turns, which can't be Evaded or Resisted.

Reinforcement Ability: Enforcer of the First Order

Enter Battle: Kylo Ren's Command Shuttle removes 20% Turn Meter from enemy ships when they are called in as Reinforcements. Kylo Ren's Command Shuttle gains 20% Turn Meter when an enemy reinforces, other First Order allies gain half of that amount.
