Logray

Sage Ewok Support who can Daze enemies and grant Ewok allies many buffs


Light Side Support Ewok


Basic Attack: Mystical Strike

Deal Physical damage to target enemy and gain Tenacity Up for 2 turns. If an Ewok ally calls Logray to Assist, they also gain Tenacity Up for 2 turns.

Special Attack : Hypnotize (cooldown: 3)

Dispel all buffs and remove 100% Turn Meter from target enemy. Inflict Daze on all enemies for 2 turns. This attack can't be Evaded.

Special Attack : Prophetic Visions (cooldown: 4)

All allies gain 20% Turn Meter. Ewok allies gain Foresight, Advantage, and Offense Up for 2 turns.

Unique Ability : Shaman's Insight

Whenever an Ewok ally scores a Critical Hit, Logray gains 5% Turn Meter. While Logray is active, whenever an Ewok ally scores a Critical Hit, that ally gains Health Up for 2 turns, and then all Ewok allies with Health Up recover 10% Health.
