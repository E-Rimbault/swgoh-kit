Eeth Koth

Stunning Jedi Support extremely effective against Droids


Light Side Support Leader Galactic Republic Jedi Jedi Vanguard


Basic Attack: Breaching Strike

Deal Physical damage to target enemy with a 50% chance to inflict Defense Down for 3 turns. If the target is a Droid, this attack has a 100% chance.

Special Attack: Force Push (cooldown: 3)

Deal Special damage to target enemy with a 75% chance to Stun for 1 turn and a 100% chance to inflict Ability Block for 3 turns if the target is a Droid.

Leader Ability: Stalwart Jedi Defender

Jedi allies gain 60 Defense.

Unique Ability: Anti-Droid Specialist

Eeth Koth has +35% Critical Chance and +20% Critical Damage when attacking Droids.
