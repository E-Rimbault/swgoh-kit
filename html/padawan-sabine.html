Padawan Sabine Wren

Trained by the legendary Ahsoka Tano in the ways of the Force, Padawan Sabine Wren
utilizes both her Master's Jedi training and her family's Mandalorian armor to defeat her foes


Light Side Attacker Mandalorian Spectre Unaligned Force User


Basic Attack: Practiced Swing

Sabine gains +20% Armor Penetration for 1 turn (stacking, max 60%). Deal Physical damage to target enemy. This ability gains 15% Critical Chance for each ally below 100% health.

Special Attack : Frantic Strike (cooldown: 3)

Deal Physical damage to target enemy and inflict Pinned for 2 turns. Then deal Physical damage to all enemies and inflict Off Balance on them for 1 turn. Enemies critically hit by this attack gain another stack of Off Balance for 1 turn (can't be resisted or evaded).

Off Balance: Lose 10% Health and 10% Turn Meter if damaged by an attack.

Special Attack : Fallback Tactic (cooldown: 3)

Deal Physical damage twice to target enemy. For each buff and debuff on the target enemy, this attack gains 10% Critical Damage (max 100%). Gains 5% Critical Chance per Mandalorian ally, doubled for Spectre allies. Remove 25% Turn Meter plus 5% per Relic level for each critical hit.

Unique Ability : Sentinel of Lothal

Padawan Sabine Wren has +70% counter chance and +25% Critical Chance and Critical Damage.

If there is a Light Side Mandalorian ally in the Leader slot, whenever Sabine uses a Special ability, she gains a stack of Ancestral Resolve (max 100) for the rest of battle.

Whenever another Spectre ally uses a Special ability, Sabine gains 1 stack of Force Connection (doubled if Ahsoka).

Whenever Sabine gains a stack of Force Connection from another Spectre ally, she gains another stack.

When Ahsoka uses an Ultimate ability, Sabine gains 10 stacks of Force Connection and 5% Mastery.

At 20 stacks of Force Connection and every 10 afterward, Sabine gains +50% Critical Damage.

Omicron Boost (Territory Wars):

Light Side Mandalorian allies gain +50% Defense, +50% Offense and +30 Speed (doubled for Spectre).

Sabine also gains +2% Critical Damage and Defense Penetration when gaining Ancestral Resolve or Force Connection.

If Ahsoka is Leader, Sabine starts with 20 stacks of Force Connection.

When Ahsoka uses a Special ability, she gains +10% Ultimate Charge.

When Ezra Bridger (Exile) gains Purrgil Migration, he gains +5 additional stacks.

When a Spectre ally loses Exile, they gain +300% Offense for 2 turns.
