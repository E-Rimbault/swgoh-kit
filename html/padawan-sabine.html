Padawn Sabine Wren

Trained by the legendary Ahsoka Tano in the ways of the Force, Padawan Sabine Wren
utilizes both her Master's Jedi training and her family's Mandalorian armor to defeat her foes


Light Side Attacker Mandalorian Spectre Unaligned Force User


Basic Attack: Practiced Swing

Sabine gains +20% Armor Penetration for 1 turn (stacking, max 60%). Deal Physical damage to target enemy.
This ability gains 15% Critical Chance for each ally below 100% health.

1st Special: Frantic Strike (cooldown: 3)

Deal Physical damage to target enemy and inflict Pinned for 2 turns. Then deal Physical damage to all enemies and inflict Off Balance on them for 1 turn. Enemies that are critically hit by this attack are inflicted with another stack of Off Balance for 1 turn, which can't be evaded or resisted.

Off Balance: Lose 10% Health and 10% Turn Meter if damaged by an attack

2nd Special: Fallback Tactic (cooldown: 3)

Deal Physical damage twice to target enemy. For each buff and debuff on the target enemy this attack gains 10% Critical Damage (max 100%). This attack gains 5% Critical Chance for each Mandalorian ally, doubled for Spectre allies. Remove 25% Turn Meter and an additional 5% Turn Meter per Relic Amplifier Level for each critical hit scored.

Unique Ability: Sentinel of Lothal

Padawan Sabine Wren has +70% counter chance and +25% Critical Chance and Critical Damage. If there is a Light Side Mandalorian ally in the Leader slot, whenever Sabine uses a Special ability, she gains a stack of Ancestral Resolve (max 100) for the rest of battle, which can't be copied, dispelled, or prevented.

Whenever another Spectre ally uses a Special ability, Sabine gains 1 stack of Force Connection, doubled if the ally is Ahsoka Tano, for the rest of battle, which can't be copied, dispelled, or prevented.

Whenever Sabine gains a stack of Force Connection from another Spectre ally, she gains an additional stack of Force Connection.

Whenever Ahsoka Tano uses an Ultimate ability, Sabine gains 10 stacks of Force Connection and 5% Mastery for the rest of battle, which can't be copied, dispelled, or prevented.

At 20 stacks of Force Connection, and every 10 stacks after, Sabine gains 50% Critical Damage for the rest of battle.

While in Territory Wars: Light Side Mandalorian allies have +50% Defense, Offense, and +30 Speed, doubled for Spectre allies.

Whenever Sabine gains a stack of Ancestral Resolve or Force Connection, she also gains 2% Critical Damage and Defense Penetration.

If the ally in the Leader slot is Ahsoka Tano, Sabine starts the battle with 20 stacks of Force Connection.

Whenever Ahsoka Tano uses a Special ability, she gains 10% Ultimate charge.

Whenever Ezra Bridger (Exile) gains Purrgil Migration, he gains an additional 5 stacks of Purrgil Migration.

Whenever a Spectre ally loses Exile, they gain 300% Offense for 2 turns.
