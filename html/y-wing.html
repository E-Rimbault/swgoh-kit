Rebel Y-wing

Sturdy Rebel Tank that can disable enemy Protection


Light Side Tank Rebel


Basic Attack: SW-4 Ion Cannons

Deal Physical damage to target enemy. The Rebel Y-wing gains Taunt until the start of its next turn if it didn't already have it.

Special Attack : Ion Torpedoes (cooldown: 3)

Dispel all buffs on target enemy and inflict Shield Disruption for 2 turns. Then, deal Physical damage to them. The Y-wing gains Taunt for 2 turns.

Shield Disruption: Protection is disabled, immune to Protection Up and bonus Protection

Unique Ability : Chempat Shield Generator

Rebel allies recover 20% Protection whenever a Rebel ally critically hits an enemy.

If the allied Capital Ship is Rebel, the Y-wing has +30% Potency and a 35% chance to assist other Rebel allies when they attack out of turn (limit once per turn).

Reinforcement Ability: Rebel Tracking Computer

Enter Battle: Rebel allies gain 3% Critical Chance for each Rebel ally until the Rebel Y-wing is defeated.
