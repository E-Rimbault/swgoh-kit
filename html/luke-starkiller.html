Starkiller (Luke Concept)

The child of a legendary warrior whose humble journey from a sun-scorched world ignited a thrilling adventure to restore peace to the galaxy


Light Side Support Rebel Unaligned Force User


Basic Attack: Laser-Sword Strike

Dispel all buffs and deal Physical damage to target enemy. If the ally in the Leader slot is Commander Luke Skywalker, during Starkiller (Luke Concept)'s turn, he gains 20% Turn Meter.

Special Attack : Son of the Suns (cooldown: 3)

Deal Physical damage to target enemy and inflict Buff Immunity for 2 turns. If the ally in the Leader slot is Commander Luke Skywalker, he assists and Starkiller (Luke Concept) inflicts Shared Fate on that enemy for 2 turns, which can't be copied, dispelled, or resisted. If Commander Luke Skywalker has Call to Action, he assists a second time and The Grip of Tython's cooldown is reduced by 1.

Shared Fate: When this enemy receives damage, 20% of that damage is dealt to all other enemies as True damage; this damage can't defeat them; only one enemy can be inflicted with Shared Fate at a time

Special Attack : The Grip of Tython (cooldown: 4)

Deal Physical damage to all enemies and Ability Block them for 1 turn.

If the ally in the Leader slot is Commander Luke Skywalker and there are no Galactic Legend allies, dispel all debuffs on Rebel allies, and enemies critically hit by this ability lose 20% Turn Meter. If any debuff was dispelled on Starkiller (Luke Concept), Rebel allies gain 25% Offense (stacking, max 100%) until the end of battle.

Unique Ability : The Force of Others

Rebel allies have +15% Critical Chance and +25% Max Health and Max Protection, doubled if Commander Luke Skywalker is the ally in the Leader slot.

If the ally in the Leader slot is Commander Luke Skywalker and there are no Galactic Legends: Whenever a Rebel ally critically hits the target enemy, that enemy is Exposed for 2 turns. Whenever an enemy with Shared Fate uses a Special ability, increase the cooldowns of all other enemies by 1. The first time an ally gains Guard, Starkiller (Luke Concept) and all Rebel Attacker allies without Guard gain it for 2 turns.

Whenever Commander Luke Skywalker gains Call to Action, all Rebel allies gain Call to Action for 1 turn.

Omicron Boost : While in Grand Arenas, if Commander Luke Skywalker is in the allied Leader slot and there are no Galactic Legends: Whenever a Rebel ally attacks out of turn, they have +50% Defense Penetration. Whenever a Rebel ally is Ability Blocked, they dispel it and gain Protection Up (50%, stacking) for 2 turns and 25% Turn Meter.

Enemies with Shared Fate can't gain bonus Turn Meter and whenever they start their turn, Starkiller (Luke Concept) gains 50% Turn Meter.
