Ahsoka Tano's Jedi Starfighter

Versatile Jedi Attacker that uses dispels and buffs to adapt to the battle.


Light Side Attacker Galactic Republic Jedi

Ahsoka Tano (Snips)

Basic Attack: Reflexive Shot

Deal Physical damage to target enemy with a 70% chance to gain Offense Up for 2 turns.

Target Lock: Grant another random ally Offense Up for 2 turns

Crew Ability - Ahsoka Tano (Snips) : Turnabout (cooldown: 3)

Dispel all debuffs on Ahsoka's Starfighter and gain Protection Up (50%) for 2 turns. If any debuffs were dispelled this way, Ahsoka's Starfighter and all other debuffed allies gain Retribution for 2 turns.

Special Attack : Shut Down (cooldown: 3)

Deal Physical damage to target enemy and Dispel all buffs on them. This ability's cooldown is reset and Ahsoka's Starfighter gains 50% Turn Meter whenever an enemy Reinforces.

Reinforcement Ability: Cunning Maneuver

Enter Battle: Dispel all buffs on target enemy and gain 20% Max Health for each buff dispelled.
