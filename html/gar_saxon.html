Gar Saxon

Leader who grants Empire allies Counter Chance and Assists them when they Counter Attack.


Dark Side Tank Leader Empire Mandalorian Crew Member

Ship: Gauntlet Starfighter



Basic Attack: On the Hunt

Deal Physical damage to target enemy with a 70% chance to inflict Speed Down for 2 turns. If the target already had Speed Down, reduce Gar Saxon's cooldowns by 1.

Special Attack : Calculated Ambush (cooldown: 5)

Deal Physical damage to all enemies, dealing double damage to enemies with less than 50% Turn Meter. Enemies that had at least 50% Turn Meter lose 30% Turn Meter

Leader Ability: Mandalorian Retaliation

Empire allies gain 50% Counter Chance and 40% Defense. Whenever an Empire ally uses a Basic attack, they recover 5% Health

Unique Ability : Viceroy's Vengeance

Whenever another ally attacks during an enemy turn, Gar Saxon has a 100% chance to Assist.
