Bistan

Wild Rebel Attacker who gains Frenzy, debuffs enemies, and removes Turn Meter


Light Side Attacker Rebel Rebel Fighter Rogue One Crew Member


Basic Attack: Blast 'Em

Deal Physical damage to target enemy with a 70% chance to inflict Damage Over Time for 3 turns.

Special Attack : Frenzy (cooldown: 7)

Bistan gains Frenzy for 4 turns and all other allies gain 20% Turn Meter.

Frenzy: Whenever another ally uses a Special ability, this unit gains 100% Turn Meter.

Special Attack : Gunner Tactics (cooldown: 3)

Deal Physical damage to target enemy and remove Turn Meter equal to Bistan's Potency.

Unique Ability : Amped Up

Bistan gains 10% Potency for each Rebel ally and each debuffed enemy.
