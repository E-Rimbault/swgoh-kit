Resistance Trooper

Opportunistic Resistance attacker that can deal heavy damage to debuffed enemies


Light Side Attacker Resistance Crew Members


Basic Attack: Flush Out

Deal Physical damage to target enemy with a 65% chance to Expose them for 3 turns.

Special Attack : Opportune Strike (cooldown: 4)

Deal Physical damage to target enemy and Dispel all buffs on them with a 50% chance of inflicting Speed Down for 2 turns. This ability's cooldown is reduced by 2 if the target is suffering any negative status effects.

Unique Ability : Guerilla Tactics

Resistance Trooper has +10% Potency and gains 55% Turn Meter when an enemy who is not Exposed becomes Exposed.
