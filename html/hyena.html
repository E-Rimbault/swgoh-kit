Hyena Bomber

Robust Separatist Tank that inflicts Buzz Droids and protects allies


Dark Side Tank Separatist Droïd


Basic Attack: Precision Blast

Deal Physical damage to target enemy and dispel all debuffs on the weakest Separatist ally.

Special Attack : Synchronized Salvo (cooldown: 3)

Deal Physical damage to all enemies and inflict 1 Buzz Droid, which can't be resisted. This attack ignores Defense.

Unique Ability : Chatter

Hyena Bomber ignores Taunt, and has +50% Defense for each Separatist ally and +10% Tenacity for each Droid ally.

It also Taunts for 1 turn whenever an enemy gains a Buzz Droid or an allied Vulture Droid falls below 50% Health.

Whenever a Separatist ally is Target Locked, they gain Critical Hit Immunity for 2 turns.

Reinforcement Ability: Photoreceptors

Enter Battle : Separatist allies have +25% Accuracy.

Additionally, they gain Speed Up and Critical Hit Immunity for 2 turns.
