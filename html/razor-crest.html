Razor Crest

Tenacious Bounty Hunter attacker with Breach synergy.


Light Side Attacker Bounty Hunter Cargo Ship Scoundrel
The Mandalorian (Beskar Armor)


Basic Attack: Most Wanted

Deal Physical damage to target enemy. If the enemy is Breached, inflict Marked for 2 turns, which can't be evaded or resisted.

Crew Ability - The Mandalorian (Beskar Armor) : Tenacious Pilot

Whenever another Bounty Hunter ally uses a Special ability, they first inflict Breach on the selected target for 2 turns, which can't be evaded or resisted.

The first time allies inflict Breach 6 times while Razor Crest is active, Razor Crest gains +50% Critical Damage for the rest of the battle and active Bounty Hunter allies gain +25% Offense and +25 Speed for the rest of the battle.

If a Marked enemy is defeated, reduce the cooldowns of active allied Bounty Hunter ships by 1.

While Razor Crest is active, Breached enemies have -30% Critical Damage.

Special Attack : Stalling Strafe (cooldown: 3)

Deal Physical damage to target enemy and inflict Breach for 2 turns. If the target already had Breach, inflict Buff Immunity for 2 turns. Call target other Bounty Hunter ally to assist.

Unique Ability : Trusty Transport

This ship ignores taunt effects during its turn.

At the start of battle, Razor Crest gains 5 stacks of Reinforced Hull, which can't be copied, dispelled, or prevented. The first time Razor Crest is reduced to 1% Health, it recovers 90% Health and gains 3 stacks of Reinforced Hull.

Reinforced Hull: +250% Defense per stack; lose one stack of Reinforced Hull when receiving damage

Reinforcement Ability: Pre-Empire Transponder

Enter Battle : Razor Crest gains Stealth for 2 turns and inflicts Ability Block on target enemy for 1 turn, which can't be evaded or resisted.
