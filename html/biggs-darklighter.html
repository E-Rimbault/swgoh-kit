Biggs Darklighter

Rebel Support with Critical Hit synergies who calls Assists


Light Side Support Rebel Rebel Fighter Crew Members


Basic Attack: Cadet's Aim

Deal Physical damage to target enemy with a 50% chance to gain Critical Chance Up for 2 turns. On a Critical Hit grant a random ally Critical Damage Up for 2 turns.

Special Attack : Comrade-in-Arms (cooldown: 2)

Deal Physical damage to target enemy and call an Assist. If Wedge Antilles is present he also Assists. All attacks have 75% more Critical Chance.

Unique Ability : Adrenaline Rush

Biggs Darklighter gains 100% Turn Meter and Evasion Up for 4 turns after surviving any damage from an Empire enemy or a Critical Hit from any enemy.
