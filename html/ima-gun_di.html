Ima-Gun Di

Supportive Tank that boost ally defense and grants Jedi allies Counter Chance


Light Side Support Leader Galactic Républic Jedi Jedi Vanguard


Basic Attack: Sunder

Deal Physical damage to target enemy with a 50% chance to inflict Defense Down for 2 turns. If the target is a Droid, this attack deals 100% more damage and has a 100% chance to inflict Defense Down.

Special Attack: Rebuke (cooldown: 3)

Deal Physical damage to target enemy and grant all allies Defense Up for 3 turns.

Leader Ability: Jedi Strategist

All allies gain 30 Defense and Jedi allies gain 35% Counter Chance and 25% Counter Damage.

Unique Ability: Last Stand

Ima-Gun Di recovers 15% of his Max Health each turn for 3 turns whenever he suffers a Critical Hit.
