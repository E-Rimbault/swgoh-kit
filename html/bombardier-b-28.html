B-28 Extinction-class Bomber

Aggressive Sith Tank with a passive Taunt and self-preservation tactics


Dark Side Tank Sith Sith Empire
Sith Marauder


Basic Attack: Laser Barrage

Deal Physical damage to the target enemy. On a Critical Hit, the B-28 gains Defense Up for 2 turns.

Crew Ability - Sith Marauder : Bombing Run (cooldown: 3)

Deal Physical damage to all enemies and place Concussion Mines on them. After 1 turn, these Concussion Mines explode.

Concussion Mine: Deals damage equal to 10% of the target's Max Health and Dazes the target for 2 turns when it explodes. Can't be Dispelled.

Unique Ability : High-Value Target

The first time another Sith ally takes damage and any time another Sith ally drops below 100% Health, the B-28 gains Taunt for 2 turns. While Taunting, the B-28 gains +25% Critical Avoidance.

Whenever a Sith ally takes damage or evades an attack from an enemy, there is an 80% chance to place a Concussion Mine on that enemy. The B-28 recovers 15% Protection any time a Concussion Mine explodes on an enemy.

Reinforcement Ability: Bomber Backup

Enter Battle : Place 2 Concussion Mines on all enemies, which can't be Evaded. After 1 turn, these Concussion Mines explode. While the B-28 is active, Sith allies recover 5% Protection any time a Concussion Mine explodes on an enemy.

Concussion Mine: Deals damage equal to 10% of the target's Max Health and Dazes the target for 2 turns when it explodes. Can't be Dispelled.
