Bodhi Rook

Clever Rebel Support who spots enemy units for his allies


Light Side Support Rebel Rebel Fighter Rogue One Crew Member


Basic Attack: Call Targets

Deal Physical damage to target enemy and inflict Evasion Down for 2 turns.

Special Attack : Spotter (cooldown: 4)

Inflict target enemy with Evasion Down for 2 turns, which can't be Evaded. Target other ally gains Offense Up and Potency Up for 2 turns and is called to Assist. If the target ally is a Rebel, Bodhi gains 30% Turn Meter.

Special Attack : Intercept Communications (cooldown: 4)

Remove 10% Turn Meter from all enemies (doubled on Empire enemies) which can't be Evaded. All allies gain 10% Turn Meter (doubled on Rebel allies).

Unique Ability :

While Bodhi is active, Rebel allies with Offense Up also gain +50% Defense. At the end of each of his turns, Bodhi grants Offense Up for 2 turns to a random ally who doesn't have it.
