Kit Fisto

Versatile Jedi Attacker with multi-attack, Counter Chance, and a variety of ally buffs


Light Side Attacker Leader Galactic Republic Jedi Jedi Vanguard


Basic Attack: Lightsaber Mastery

Deal Physical damage to target enemy with a 30% chance to attack again. Kit Fisto has a 50% chance to gain 15% Turn Meter with each attack.

Special Attack : Turn the Tide (cooldown: 4)

Deal Physical damage to all enemies and grant all allies Potency Up for 3 turns.

Leader Ability: Jedi Protector

All allies gain 25% Tenacity and Jedi allies gain 45 Defense.

Unique Ability : Superior Bladework

Kit Fisto has 35% Counter Chance and +20% Offense.
